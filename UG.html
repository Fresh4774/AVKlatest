Science is a systematic and evidence-based study of the natural world, seeking to understand its laws and principles. It involves observation, experimentation, and analysis to unravel the mysteries of the universe and answer fundamental questions. Through scientific discoveries, humanity has made tremendous advancements in technology, medicine, and our understanding of the cosmos. Science continues to inspire curiosity and drive innovation, shaping our world and shaping the future of human civilization. Its impact on society is profound, fostering progress and empowering us to tackle the challenges of tomorrow.

Commerce is the study of economic activities related to the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services. It encompasses a wide range of fields, including finance, marketing, accounting, and management. Through commerce, businesses thrive, economies grow, and global trade expands. It plays a crucial role in shaping modern society, driving entrepreneurship, and fostering financial stability. By understanding the principles of commerce, individuals and organizations can make informed decisions that drive prosperity and sustainable growth in the interconnected world.

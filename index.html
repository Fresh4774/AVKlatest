About Us

AVK Trust

Our aim is to amalgamate all aspects to make the students as true citizens. This requires inculcating the true spirit of education in students, imparting knowledge in the right way, bookish as well as practical and cementing this knowledge with humanism, patriotism and selfless knowledge that would serve their purpose in all walks of life and strengthening the same with the qualities of humbleness, modesty and simplicity which Dr. S. Radhakrishnan often reiterated in his addresses to the students and educationalists. It was formed in the year 2000 under the dynamic leadership and initiation of Dr. Rajeev Rai, with a motto to “We educate a child in true sense”. It imparts regular and professional courses in Commerce, Computer Applications, Pharmacy, Nursing and Management with the steering objective of reaching out to the enterprising students across the country.
